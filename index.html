About

Hi everyone! My name is Bhavya, and today I will tell you about my hobby. My Hobby is Drawing. Or mostly, doodling. I love doodling because it helps me express my feelings. Drawing is fun, because you get to observe things around you, and notice things you haven't noticed before. Drawing helps you focus on the thing you want to copy on your paper without any distractions. It also helps you increase your levels of imagination. With drawing, you can not only observe and draw, you can create things that can soon be world - famous. As Walt Disney says, "It's kind of fun to do the impossible." Many people also say, "Put your feelings into a drawing." Drawing is a very fun hobby.
